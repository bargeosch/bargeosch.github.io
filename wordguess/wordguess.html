How to Play

Enter a letter that you think may occur in the puzzle.
You will receive 30 points for each correct letter.
If the letter occurs more than once you will receive 30 points per occurrance.
If the letter does not occur in the puzzle 10 points will be subtracted from your score.

Good Luck!
