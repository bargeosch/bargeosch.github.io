Word Guess Game is supposed to be a mash-up between Jeopardy and Wheel of Fortune. Trivia questions where you can determine the answer by guessing the letters. Give it a try to see what I mean.

FYI - This game uses a cookie to remember your score and what puzzles you've solved. I set the cookie expiration date for 2 days. After that you lose your progress. Sorry.
